~A "scary" description of Halloween~

Halloween or Hallowe'en, also known as Allhalloween,All Hallows' Eve,All Saints' Eve.... is a celebration observed in a number of countries on 31 October, it is the time in the liturgical year dedicated to remembering the dead, including saints (hallows), martyrs, and all the faithful departed(although people just go trick-or-treat and ignores everything else).Halloween activities include trick-or-treating (or the related guising), attending Halloween costume parties, carving pumpkins into jack-o'-lanterns......
